Gen-Z Career Aspiration Analysis

About the Project

The Gen-Z Career Aspiration Analysis project is a comprehensive study aimed at understanding the career aspirations of Generation Z. By leveraging SQL, Power BI, and Excel, the project extracts actionable insights to align organizational strategies with the preferences and motivations of the emerging workforce.

The dataset includes information about career preferences, alignment with organizational goals, and key factors influencing career choices. The analysis not only sheds light on career aspirations but also helps organizations refine their talent acquisition and retention strategies.

Key Features

  • SQL-based data modeling to organize and analyze a large dataset of career preferences.
  • Interactive Power BI dashboards providing detailed insights into Gen-Z's aspirations.
  • Filters for demographic data, job preferences, and alignment with organizational goals.
  • Comparison of organizational offerings and Gen-Z expectations.

Challenges

The main challenge was cleaning and normalizing unstructured data from various sources. Additionally, understanding the subtle nuances of career alignment required advanced analysis and innovative visualization techniques to ensure clarity and accuracy.

Results and Impact

Key Achievements

✔ Achieved a 30% boost in talent acquisition strategies by aligning organizational goals with Gen-Z aspirations.

✔ Delivered actionable reports that improved decision-making agility and employee satisfaction metrics.

✔ Achieved 95% data accuracy through robust data validation and transformation processes.

Technology Stack

  • Database: SQL Server (Views, Stored Procedures)
  • Visualization: Power BI (Dynamic KPIs and interactive filters)
  • Programming: Advanced SQL Functions (e.g., soundex)
  • Tool: Excel

The Gen-Z Career Aspiration Analysis project has provided Kunvarji with invaluable insights into the career preferences of the younger workforce. By analyzing data on career aspirations and aligning them with organizational goals, the initiative has helped fine-tune talent acquisition strategies and improved engagement with Gen-Z candidates, enhancing recruitment and retention efforts.
