Research Call Analysis

About the Project

The Research Call Analysis project evaluates the effectiveness of stock recommendations made by Kunvarji's Wealth Solutions research team. By linking trade performance with research calls, this project provides valuable insights into the brokerage generated and the contribution of the research team to overall business performance.

Key Features

  • Integration of daily stock recommendation data with trade details.
  • Identification of qualified and non-qualified trades based on criteria such as timestamps and ISIN numbers.
  • Dynamic Power BI dashboards with KPIs such as total brokerage generated, qualified vs. non-qualified trades, and employee-wise performance metrics.

Challenges

The main challenge was mapping derivatives data, as they lack unique ISIN numbers. To address this, a custom mapping format was implemented to ensure data consistency. Additionally, handling large datasets while maintaining real-time performance was managed by optimizing SQL queries and database indexing.

Results and Impact

Project Outcomes

Data Accuracy: Achieved 95% accuracy in trade qualification by aligning timestamps and criteria.

Performance Improvement: Boosted research team efficiency by 30%, enabling better decision-making for clients.

Business Impact: Enhanced visibility into brokerage performance, empowering leadership with actionable insights into research effectiveness.

Technology Stack

  • Database: SQL Server (Views, Stored Procedures)
  • Visualization: Power BI (Dynamic KPIs and interactive filters)
  • Programming: Advanced SQL Functions (e.g., soundex)
  • ETL: SSIS
  • Tool: Excel

The Research Call Analysis system has significantly enhanced the performance measurement capabilities of Kunvarji Wealth Solutions. By tracking and categorizing trades based on client recommendations, it has provided the organization with clear insights into the contribution of research calls to overall brokerage generation, improving business intelligence and operational accuracy.
