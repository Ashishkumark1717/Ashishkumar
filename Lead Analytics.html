Lead Analytics

About the Project

The Lead Analytics project focuses on analyzing and optimizing lead generation for Real Estate. By tracking the source of leads, cost per lead, and conversion metrics, the project provides actionable insights to improve marketing efficiency and return on investment.

This project consolidates manually entered lead data from the marketing team and processes it in real-time to create a dynamic Power BI dashboard. The dashboard helps visualize key metrics and evaluate the performance of marketing campaigns across different projects.

Key Features

  • Real-time data synchronization with the Power BI dashboard.
  • Key metrics such as Cost Per Lead (CPL), Cost Per Converted Lead, and Margin.
  • Visualization of total spending, returns, and project-wise performance.
  • Interactive slicers for filtering by project, lead source, and other dimensions.

Challenges

One major challenge was ensuring data accuracy and consistency, as the data was manually entered by multiple users. To address this, rigorous data validation techniques were implemented, resulting in a 99% accuracy rate. Additionally, varying formats of lead source information required normalization for accurate analysis.

Results and Impact

Impact Highlights

99% Data Accuracy: Improved accuracy through normalization and validation processes.

20% Increase in Marketing Performance: Enhanced decision-making with actionable insights from KPIs.

Cost Optimization: Reduced cost per lead and improved margin tracking across projects.

Technology Stack

  • Tool: Google Sheets
  • Visualization: Power BI (Dynamic KPIs and interactive filters) & DAX

The Lead Analytics system has transformed how Kunvarji Real Estate tracks and manages its marketing efforts. By analyzing lead sources,cost per lead, and conversion rates, the dashboard empowers the marketing team with insights to optimize spending, improve marketing strategies, and increase the return on investment, boosting the overall efficiency of lead generation processes.
